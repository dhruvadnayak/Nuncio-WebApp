Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

What is Bulk Mailing (Mass mail dispatcher)

Bulk Mailing is a process of sending very large quantities of emails at once. It is done with the help of mass email service providers. This process helps reduce time as well as cost for sending a huge number of emails to different recipients.

How to Send Mass Mails?

Step 1) Download and install any of the above-listed bulk email service provider software Step 2) Login or Sign Up using your details Step 3) Make sure you have your email list ready in a .csv file format Step 4) Now, create an email campaign and upload your email list Step 5) Create and compose your email Step 6) Send or Schedule your email Step 7) Click Send if you want to send emails
